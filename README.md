# Blackjack Simulator with Strategy Advisor

**[Play Now](https://blackjack-sim-advisor.netlify.app/)**

Practice blackjack and learn optimal strategy with real-time guidance. The strategy advisor shows you the mathematically correct play for every hand.

## Features

- **Real-time strategy advice** - Get recommendations for hit, stand, double, or split based on basic strategy
- **Learn why** - Each recommendation explains the reasoning behind the correct play
- **Track your progress** - See your win rate and session statistics
- **Practice splits and doubles** - Full support for all standard blackjack actions
- **Mobile friendly** - Play on any device

## Basic Strategy Quick Reference

**Always:**
- Hit on 8 or less
- Stand on 17+
- Double on 11

**vs Dealer 2-6 (weak):**
- Stand on 12-16
- Double on 10

**vs Dealer 7-A (strong):**
- Hit on 12-16

**Pairs:**
- Always split Aces and 8s
- Never split 10s and 5s
